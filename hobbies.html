Hobbies

Homeschooling my kids is the biggest "hobby" that I do. I take it very seriously because my kids health, wellbeing, and education are my top priorities. I've even taken it upon myself to set up an area where they can study, just like you would have in a public school!

Raising chickens! I enjoy raising random chickens and watching them grow to produce food for my family. The work they require is well worth the effort, and I find myself buying more of them even when I wasn't planning on it.

Renovating my house is another thing I enjoy doing. There are lots of areas that had to be redone, and I think I have done a fairly good job considering we just tentatively sold the house for a lot!
